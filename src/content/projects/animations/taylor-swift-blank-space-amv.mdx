    abbreviation: >
        "Blank Space" is a satirical song that exaggerates the media's portrayal
        of her as a dramatic and vengeful woman who cycles through men. Instead
        of fighting the public perception, Swift leans into the character, playing
        a woman who is fully aware of her reputation and the media's narrative
        about her love life. The song is an electropop track that critiques the
        public's double standards regarding women who date frequently.
